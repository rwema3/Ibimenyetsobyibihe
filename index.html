UBUHANUZI

Impinduka zidasanzwe ziri kuba buri munsi, ariko Yesu aduha ibyiringiro ati: "Nimuhumure nanesheje iyi si." twigira hamwe ijambo ry'Imana tunareba ibyaduka mu isi by'ubuhanuzi buri gusohora kugirango bitubashishe Kwitegura Yesu Kristo ugiye kugaruka bidatinze. MARANATHA!

Luka 21:34-36.

UBUTUMWA BWIZA

Ubutumwa bwiza nibwo mutima w'ubutumwa bwa malayika wa gatatu. Twubwiga mukumenya uko umunyabyaha yongera kwiyunga n'Imana kandi akagirana amahoro nayo binyuze mu kubabarirwa ibicumuro n'ibyaha bye, ngo tubone uko twishimira ibyiringiro byo kuzabona ubwiza bw’Imana. MARANATHA!

Abaroma 5:1-4

UBUZIMA BUZIMA

Imibiri yacu n'insengero z'Imana kandi kugirango ibya Mwuka byubakwe muri twe, bisaba kuba twayirinze. Umwuka mwiza, umucyo w’izuba, kwirinda, ikiruhuko, imyitozo ngororamubiri, imirire iboneye, gukoresha amazi meza, kwiringira ubushobozi bw’Imana, iyi ni yo miti nyakuri. Buri muntu akwiriye kumenya imiti ikoreshwa yo mu byaremwe n’uburyo bwo kuyikoresha. MARANATHA!
1 Abakorinto 6:19-20.
